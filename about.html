Who We Are

PawSome is a heartfelt initiative started by animal lovers who believe every pet deserves a forever home. We rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome pets across all species — from dogs and cats to birds, bunnies, and beyond.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, PawSome began as a university project with one mission: to reduce the number of homeless pets. Over time, it evolved into a full-fledged online platform where users can browse, connect, and adopt adorable companions. We've connected 150+ pets with loving families so far — and we're just getting started.

Our Mission

We want to make adoption simple, joyful, and accessible. By blending technology with compassion, we’re here to help people fall in love with their next best friend.
